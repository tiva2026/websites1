TIPS & SECRETS

Everything you need to find every collectible, solve every puzzle, and uncover every hidden secret in Mixtape.

Essential Tips

Start your playthrough right

🎧

Use Headphones

The game's audio design includes subtle clues in the music and ambient sounds. Stereo headphones help you catch directional audio hints during exploration.

💾

Manual Save Often

The game has 10 manual save slots. Use them before major dialogue choices — some decisions permanently lock you out of content or affect endings.

💬

Exhaust All Dialogue

Talk to every character multiple times. New dialogue options appear after story events, and some collectibles are only available through conversation.

🔍

Look for the Shimmer

Interactable objects have a subtle shimmer effect. It's easy to miss in bright areas — adjust your brightness if you're having trouble spotting items.

⏰

Take Your Time

Except for the Chapter 4 escape sequence, there are no time limits. Explore thoroughly before advancing the story — some areas become inaccessible.

🎭

Choices Matter

Your dialogue choices affect friendship levels, available scenes, and endings. There's no "wrong" choice, but being consistent with your character helps.

Puzzle Solutions

Stuck? Here's how to solve every puzzle

🧩

The Record Sleeve (Chapter 3)

Finding the hidden message at Spinning Records

▼

The Puzzle

Mr. Hendricks hints that the next clue is hidden in a specific album. You need to find it among hundreds of records.

Solution

  1. Go to the "Classic Rock" section on the back wall
  2. Look for albums from 1972 (check the spine labels)
  3. Find "Midnight Highway" by The Drifters
  4. The correct copy has a small star drawn on the spine
  5. Open the sleeve to find the message inside
Hint from Mr. Hendricks: "The highway at midnight... that was Eddie's favorite drive. 1972 was a good year for music."
🧩

The Mill Escape (Chapter 4)

Navigating out before the collapse

▼

The Puzzle

After finding the clue, the floor starts collapsing. You have 60 seconds to escape.

Solution (With Map)

  1. Follow the glowing markers on your map
  2. Take the main staircase down
  3. Exit through the loading dock (marked on map)

Solution (Without Map)

  1. Follow Derek — he remembers the layout
  2. When he says "left," go left immediately
  3. Jump over the collapsed beam when prompted
  4. Exit through the window Derek breaks
Tip: Find the map in the ground floor office BEFORE going upstairs. It makes the escape much easier.
🧩

The Radio Frequency (Chapter 6)

Tuning to the right station

▼

The Puzzle

The old radio equipment needs to be tuned to a specific frequency to reveal the hidden message on the tape.

Solution

  1. Check the DJ's logbook on the desk — it mentions "our lucky number"
  2. Look at the calendar on the wall — it shows July 7, 1972
  3. The date gives you the frequency: 77.2 FM
  4. Tune the radio dial to 77.2
  5. Play the tape to hear the hidden message
Easter Egg: If you tune to 92.1 FM first, you'll hear a snippet of the game's theme song.
🧩

The Trail Markers (Chapter 10)

Following Eddie's musical clues through the woods

▼

The Puzzle

Eddie left markers in the woods that correspond to songs on the tape. You need to play the right song at each marker to reveal the path.

Solution

  1. First Marker (Sun Symbol): Play "Summer Daze" → Head East toward the sunrise
  2. Second Marker (Star Carving): Play "Starlight Serenade" → Follow the North Star carving
  3. Third Marker (Winding Path): Play "Memory Lane" → Take the overgrown path
  4. Final Marker (Oak Tree): Play "The Last Song" → You've arrived at the clearing
Tip: If you don't have all the cassettes, the characters will hum the tunes instead, but you'll miss some optional dialogue.

Easter Eggs & Secrets

Hidden content for dedicated explorers

🎹

Mr. Hendricks' Piano

Return to Spinning Records at night after Chapter 6. You'll find Mr. Hendricks playing piano and can learn his full backstory — he was Eddie's best friend.

📻

Secret Radio Broadcast

In Chapter 6, if you broadcast a message before leaving the station, someone responds in Chapter 8. It's a survivor from Eddie's original group.

🎮

Arcade Easter Egg

In Chapter 3, visit the arcade downtown. One of the cabinets is playable — it's a mini-game version of the developer's previous title.

🌟

Developer Room

In New Game+, return to the school music room and play the piano keys in the order: C-A-S-S-E-T-T-E. A hidden door opens to a developer thank-you room.

👻

Eddie's Ghost?

In Chapter 4, if you look out the third-floor window at exactly the right moment, you can see a figure watching from the treeline. Fans debate whether it's Eddie's ghost or foreshadowing.

🎵

Hidden Song

Collect all 10 cassettes and play them in order at the treehouse in Chapter 12. A hidden 11th track plays — Eddie's original demo recording.

🎯 Skill-Based Achievements

🏆

Thrashed

Ollie a car

▼

How to Get: During any of the skating sections, press A/X as a car approaches you to jump over it.

Tip: Look for smaller cars as these are much easier to jump over.
🏆

Smooth Shopper

Don't crash once while escaping in the shopping cart

▼

Chapter: Chapter 5: Shopping Cart Bomb

How to Get: Avoid crashing into walls or cars as you go. It's okay if you knock into some boxes in the alley or traffic cones.

🏆

Instructions Not Required

Complete minimaster assembly in under 15 seconds

▼

Chapter: Chapter 13: Cassandra's House

How to Get: Repair the minimaster quickly:

  1. Immediately flip it over
  2. Remove the battery cover
  3. Correctly insert two batteries (look for + and - icons)
  4. Replace the battery cover
  5. Turn it back over
  6. Press the 'On' button (on the left)
🏆

And It's Outta There!

Hit only home runs as Cassandra

▼

Chapter: Chapter 14: Softball

How to Get: Get the timing right when batting as Cassandra. As long as you make a connection with the ball, you'll get a home run. Use Rockford's attempts at the beginning to practice timing. If you miss, restart the chapter.

🏆

Well, Take a Look at You

Don't miss a single jump or slide while rescuing Cassandra

▼

Chapter: Chapter 27: The Run

How to Get: Make sure you jump or slide past every obstacle on your way to save Cassandra from being caught by her dad. Perfect timing required!

🏆

Paint it, Black, If You Want

Paint the entire door in under 30 seconds

▼

Chapter: Chapter 21: Door Painting

How to Get: Use the large paint roller and hold RT/R2 to do large vertical lines. Start from the bottom left corner, go up, then move the roller with a tiny overlap and repeat across the door.

Sofa King Fast

▼

Chapter: Chapter 22: Couch Moving

How to Get: Keep moving the couch to the right, using analog sticks to navigate obstacles. Move it into the blue outline by the campfire within 20 seconds.

Leaves That Are Green

▼

Chapter: Chapter 23: Leaf Sweeping

How to Get: Use RT/R2 to sweep leaves out of the circle around the campfire. Press and hold RT/R2 to charge up your sweep — this moves more leaves much further and is the fastest method.

From the Hip

How to Get: Hit all cans and bottles on the car. Focus on accuracy — one missed shot can be costly due to re-aiming time.
