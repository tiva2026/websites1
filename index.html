About Mixtape

What is this game?

Mixtape is a narrative adventure game developed by Beethoven & Dinosaur and published by Annapurna Interactive. Set in 1989, you follow four teenage friends — Rockford, Cassandra, Slater, and Debbie — as they relive memories through a collection of mixtapes on their last night together before everything changes.

The game blends exploration, dialogue, and various minigames including skateboarding, photography, and more. Each chapter unlocks memories tied to different bedrooms and locations, building toward an emotional finale. The "Front to Back" trophy requires finding every interaction across all locations.

Quick Start Tips

Before you press play

⚪

Watch for White Dots

Interactable items show a white dot. Once examined, the dot becomes hollow. Check every item for the "Front to Back" trophy.

💬

Exhaust All Dialogue

Talk to Cassandra and Slater repeatedly in each scene until they mention "should we leave" — this is required for 100% completion.

🔄

Use Chapter Select

After completing any chapter, you can replay it. The game tracks interactions across replays, so you can clean up missed items later.

👀

Look Up & Around

Some interactions are above eye level (like curtains) or behind you (like doors). Explore every angle in each room.
