📋 Guide Overview

This walkthrough covers all 30 chapters, every interaction for the "Front to Back" trophy, and all minigame achievements. Playtime: ~3-4 hours.

Chapter Select: Available after completing any chapter. The game tracks interactions across replays.

01

🏆 Thrashed — Ollie over any car

▼

The game opens with a skateboarding sequence. Navigate the streets while avoiding obstacles.

Trophy: Thrashed

Press jump to ollie over a vehicle. Smaller cars are easier. If you crash, the game rewinds so you can retry. More skating sections appear later if you miss it here.
