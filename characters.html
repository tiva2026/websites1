CHARACTER PROFILES

Meet the four friends at the heart of Mixtape. Set in 1989, their last night together becomes a journey through memories, mixtapes, and the bonds that define them.

Main Characters

The crew that makes this night unforgettable

🛹

ROCKFORD

The Protagonist • Skater

The main character whose perspective we follow throughout most of the game. Rockford is a skateboarder navigating the complexities of friendship, memories, and growing up. His room serves as the first major exploration area where the journey begins.

Nostalgic Loyal Adventurous
🎀

CASSANDRA

The Heart • Athlete

A talented softball player with a room full of trophies. Cassandra brings warmth and determination to the group. Her house becomes the second major location, featuring unique interactions including the rooftop scene and the memorable photobooth moments.

Caring Athletic Determined
🎸

SLATER

The Free Spirit • Music Lover

The laid-back member of the group with a passion for music and slushies. Slater has a secret recipe (Blue Crush, Bubblegum, Lime, and Orange) and his room is filled with guitars, a fish tank, and a lava lamp. He's always there with a quip or a moment of unexpected depth.

Relaxed Musical Witty
💜

DEBBIE

The Mystery • Rockford's Sister

Rockford's sister whose room is initially locked at the start of the game. Debbie's presence is felt throughout the story even when she's not directly on screen. Her bedroom becomes accessible in Chapter 7, revealing more about the family dynamics and shared history.

Mysterious Important Connected

Key Locations

The bedrooms and places that hold the memories

🏠

Rockford's House

The starting location featuring Rockford's bedroom, the hallway with Debbie's closed door, and the bathroom. This is where the first set of memories are explored.

Chapters 1-10 • "Goodbye, Stacey's Room" Trophy

🏡

Cassandra's House

Features Cassandra's trophy-filled room, the rooftop area, and various family photos. The curtains above the window are a commonly missed interaction.

Chapters 11-18 • "Matching The Drapes" Trophy

🏚️

The Shitty Ritz

The gang's secret hideout where they paint doors, move furniture, sweep leaves, and practice with slingshots. A place of shared memories and teamwork.

Chapters 20-24 • Multiple Minigame Trophies

🎸

Slater's House

Filled with music memorabilia, a plasma orb, fish tank, and lava lamp. The tape player here triggers important story moments.

Chapter 25 • "Eight Minute Odyssey" Trophy

Group Dynamics

How the friends connect through the story

The Core Friendship

Rockford, Cassandra, and Slater form the central trio that you interact with throughout the game. They appear together in most scenes, with dialogue options available for both friends in each location. The game tracks your interactions with them for the "Front to Back" trophy.

Exhaust Dialogue

Talk to both Cassandra and Slater until they mention "should we leave" before progressing

Scene Changes

Characters move positions after certain events — talk to them again in their new spots

Shared Memories

Each bedroom unlocks memories tied to that character's story and relationships

The Final Night

The party at the Ritz brings everyone together for the emotional finale

Character Moments

Memorable scenes and minigames featuring each character

🛹

Rockford's Moments

Skateboarding, explosions, and the run to save Cassandra

▼

Key Scenes

  • Chapter 1: Skate to Rockford's — Ollie over cars for "Thrashed" trophy
  • Chapter 11: Skate to Cassandra's — Hit all 10 cones for "Cone Island, Baby" trophy
  • Chapter 19: Skateboard Explosion — Destroy everything for "Appetite For Destruction"
  • Chapter 27: The Run — Perfect jumps and slides for "Well, Take A Look At You"
Character Note: Rockford's skateboarding sequences bookend the game, showing his journey from casual skating to a desperate run to help his friends.
🎀

Cassandra's Moments

Softball, rooftop conversations, and the photobooth

▼

Key Scenes

  • Chapter 3: The Kiss — Romantic moment with "French Connection" trophy
  • Chapter 9: Photobooth — Fun group photos and important dialogue
  • Chapter 14: Softball — Hit home runs as Cassandra for "And It's Outta There!"
  • Chapter 17: Rooftop — Assemble the Minimaster for "Instructions Not Required"
Character Note: Cassandra's athletic achievements (trophies in her room) contrast with her emotional depth shown in quieter moments.
🎸

Slater's Moments

Slushies, video stores, and party filming

▼

Key Scenes

  • Chapter 8: Ultimate Slushie — Make his secret recipe for "Grunge Metal Alchemist"
  • Chapter 26: Starlight Video — Drunk Slater knocks over cutouts and tapes
  • Chapter 29: Party at the Ritz — Film with camcorder, collect all items
  • Chapter 4: Headbanging — Rock out together in this rhythm minigame
Character Note: Slater's laid-back personality shines through his love of music, his secret slushie recipe, and his chaotic video store adventure.
💜

Debbie's Presence

The sister whose room holds secrets

▼

Key Scenes

  • Chapter 2: Her closed door in the hallway — easy to miss interaction!
  • Chapter 7: Debbie's Bedroom — Finally accessible, full of items to examine
  • Throughout: Her presence is felt in family photos and conversations
Important: The closed door to Debbie's room in Chapter 2 is one of the most commonly missed interactions for the "Front to Back" trophy. Make sure to interact with it!
